About Blood Groups

Blood groups are based on the presence or absence of certain markers called antigens on the surface of red blood cells. The most common blood groups are A, B, AB, and O.

Types of Blood Groups

  • A
  • B
  • AB
  • O

Each blood group can also be either Rh-positive or Rh-negative, depending on the presence or absence of the Rh factor.

Donate Blood

If you are eligible to donate blood, you can help save lives. Contact your local blood bank to find out how to donate.
